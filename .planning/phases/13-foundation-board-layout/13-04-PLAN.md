    Rewrite `client/src/components/hand/PlayerHand.tsx` for MTGA-style interaction:

    **Fan layout from bottom edge:**
    - Cards peek from bottom with only top ~30% visible in collapsed state
    - Container positioned at absolute bottom of parent, cards overflow downward off-screen
    - Cards fanned with slight rotation (each card rotated by `(i - center) * 3` degrees) and overlapping via negative margin
    - On hover over hand zone: cards expand upward (translateY to show full card)
    - Use Framer Motion `animate` with `whileHover` on the container, not individual cards

    **Touch support per CONTEXT:**
    - Tap hand zone to expand (toggle state)
    - Tap card to select (shows hover preview via inspectObject)
    - Tap again or drag to play
    - Long-press for full preview (existing `useLongPress` hook)
    - Tap elsewhere to deselect and collapse

    **Drag-to-play:**
    - Each card wrapped in `motion.div` with `drag="y"`, `dragConstraints={{ top: -300, bottom: 0 }}`, `dragElastic={0.3}`, `dragSnapToOrigin`
    - `onDragEnd`: check `info.offset.y < -50` (50px threshold per locked decision). If exceeded AND card is playable, dispatch PlayLand or CastSpell
    - `whileDrag={{ scale: 1.05, zIndex: 50 }}` for visual feedback

    **Playable card highlighting:**
    - When `waitingFor.type === "Priority"` and player has priority:
      - Cards that are playable get `shadow-[0_0_12px_3px_rgba(34,197,94,0.7)]` (green glow)
      - Non-playable cards get `opacity-70` (slightly dimmed)
    - For now, highlight ALL hand cards when player has priority (per research open question #2: legal action filtering may need engine support). This matches current behavior but with green glow instead of white.

    **Hover preview:**
    - On mouse enter: call `inspectObject(obj.id)` (existing behavior, CardPreview shows to side)

    Refine `client/src/components/hand/OpponentHand.tsx`:
    - Keep compact card backs fanned horizontally at top edge
    - Use smaller scale for card backs (`--card-w` * 0.6 or similar) to reduce visual weight
    - Maintain overlap via negative margin (existing behavior)
    - No functional changes needed -- just ensure styling is consistent with the new layout
