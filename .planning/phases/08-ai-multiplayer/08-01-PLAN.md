   - `pub fn choose_blockers(state: &GameState, player: PlayerId, attacker_ids: &[ObjectId]) -> Vec<(ObjectId, ObjectId)>` — for each attacker, find the best blocker: minimize damage taken. Prefer blocking with creatures that would survive (toughness > attacker power). Double-block only if needed to kill attacker. Assign deathtouch creatures to highest-value attackers.
   - `fn can_attack(state: &GameState, obj_id: ObjectId) -> bool` — not tapped, has haste or not summoning sick
   - `fn can_block(state: &GameState, blocker_id: ObjectId, attacker_id: ObjectId) -> bool` — flying/reach check, not tapped
