<objective>
Overhaul the combat system for per-creature attack target selection and multi-defender support.

Purpose: In multiplayer MTG, each attacking creature independently chooses its target (player or planeswalker). This requires changing DeclareAttackers from a flat list of attacker IDs to a list of (attacker, target) pairs, and updating blocking to only allow blocking creatures that are attacking you.

Output: Extended DeclareAttackers action, updated combat.rs and combat_damage.rs, AttackTarget enum. 2-player games work identically (all attackers target the single opponent).
</objective>
